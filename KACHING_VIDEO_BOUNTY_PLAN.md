# Kaching Football WC Video Bounty Plan

## Correct Bounty Requirement

This bounty is a video-first content submission, not a written article bounty.

The required deliverable is a short explainer video that:

- explains Kaching as a fair on-chain lottery on Solana
- explains why Solana matters for low-fee, fast, repeat participation
- breaks down the Football WC campaign in simple language
- shows a live screen recording flow: visit Kaching.vip -> sign up / connect wallet -> add funds -> buy at least one ticket
- includes the creator referral link in the video description and on-screen CTA
- is published on a public platform such as YouTube, X, TikTok, Instagram Reels, or Shorts
- is submitted to Superteam with the video link and Solana wallet address
