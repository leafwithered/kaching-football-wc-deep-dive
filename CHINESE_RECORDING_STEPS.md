# Kaching Recording Steps

Goal: record a 2-5 minute video that clearly explains Kaching and shows how to join the Football WC campaign.

## Must Cover

- Kaching is a fair on-chain lottery on Solana.
- Fairness: transparent draws, verifiable results, on-chain records, trackable proof.
- Why Solana: low fees, fast settlement, suitable for frequent small-ticket participation.
- Football WC campaign: FIFA WC 2026, pot size, ticket price, countdown, slots, leaderboard.
- Live flow: open Kaching.vip -> login -> connect wallet -> prepare funds -> buy at least 1 ticket -> show My Tickets / Results.
- Referral link appears on screen and in the video description.

## WalletConnect With Mobile Phantom

1. Open `https://kaching.vip/` on desktop.
2. Log in to Kaching.
3. Click `BUY TICKET` or the balance plus button.
4. Find `WALLETCONNECT` in the wallet list.
5. Click `CONNECT`.
6. Scan the QR code with mobile Phantom.
7. Confirm connection on the phone.

Never show or share seed phrase, private key, or recovery phrase.
