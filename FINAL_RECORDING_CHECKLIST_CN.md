# Final Recording Checklist

Before recording:

- Kaching page is logged in.
- Wallet is connected and page shows `CONNECTED WALLET`.
- Balance or funding page can be shown.
- Referral link is ready.
- No seed phrase, private key, email code, API key, or sensitive data is visible.
- Total spend stays below 3 USDC.

Video structure:

1. 10 seconds: introduce the topic.
2. 35 seconds: explain what Kaching is.
3. 25 seconds: explain why Solana fits.
4. 35 seconds: explain the Football WC campaign.
5. 90-150 seconds: show the live flow: visit, login, connect wallet, prepare funds, buy ticket.
6. 15 seconds: show My Tickets / Results / OnChain entry points.
7. 10 seconds: referral link CTA.

Publishing order:

1. Upload to YouTube.
2. Put referral link in YouTube description.
3. Post on X with YouTube link and referral link.
4. Update Superteam submission with video link and wallet address.
