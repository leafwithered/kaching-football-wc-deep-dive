Kaching is a fair on-chain lottery experience built around Solana.

The fairness angle matters. Kaching presents the experience around transparent draws, verifiable randomness, smart-contract-based ticket recording, and on-chain visibility for prize pools and results.

That does not mean users need to become blockchain analysts. The product should still feel simple: ticket, draw, result, proof.

## 0:55-1:25 Why Solana

Solana is a good fit because lottery-style apps need frequent, low-friction actions.

Users may buy small tickets, check results often, join multiple draws, and return for the next campaign moment. That works better when fees are low, transactions are fast, and the wallet experience is smooth.

Solana also makes the trust claim easier to inspect: ticket activity, pools, and results can be tied back to on-chain activity instead of only asking users to trust a web page.
